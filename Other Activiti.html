Bob Sledding

Bobsledding, also called bobsleighing, is the sport of sliding down an icy track on a special sled. It is one of the most exciting snow activities and is popular with guests who enjoy speed and adventure.

Is Skating Good Exercise?

Yes. Skating is a good full-body activity that supports cardio fitness, strength, control, and balance. It is also an enjoyable outdoor exercise that many people can include in their holiday routine.

Why Try Other Activities?
