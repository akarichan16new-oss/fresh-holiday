Prepare for the Mountain

A great ski holiday starts with the right equipment, the right energy, and the right destination.

What we need?

To enjoy skiing safely and comfortably, it is important to wear warm and practical clothing. The right ski outfit helps protect your body from cold weather and supports better movement during activity.

🧥 Ski jacket
👖 Ski pants
🧦 Ski socks
🧤 Gloves or mittens
🪖 Helmet
🥽 Goggles
🧣 Wool or fleece top
👕 Long underwear

Is skiing good?

Yes. Skiing strengthens the glutes, hamstrings, and core muscles. It also improves balance, coordination, and endurance, making it both a fun and healthy winter sport.
