Travel Tips

Before your ski trip, make sure you prepare the right clothing and equipment for a safe and comfortable holiday.

What we need?

🧥 Ski or snowboard jacket
👖 Ski pants or bibs
🧦 Ski socks
🧤 Gloves or mittens
🪖 Helmet
🥽 Goggles
👕 Long underwear
🧣 Light fleece or wool top

Skiing is good?

Yes. Skiing helps strengthen the glutes, hamstrings, and core muscles. It also improves balance, control, and endurance while giving you an exciting outdoor experience.
