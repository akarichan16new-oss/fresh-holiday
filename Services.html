Our Services

These services are prepared to support beginners, returning skiers, and experienced visitors. Each service is planned to improve safety, comfort, and enjoyment throughout the holiday.

Ski / Snowboard Lessons

Our lesson service is useful for guests who want to build confidence and feel safer on the slopes. Beginners usually need at least a week of group lessons or a series of three two-hour sessions to become more comfortable and in control.

Skill Building

Best For

Beginners and guests who want to improve technique and confidence.

Learning Style

Step-by-step guidance with practice, safety awareness, and control.

Main Benefit

Better balance, better movement, and a safer mountain experience.

🎿 Group lessons for beginners and improving skiers
🕒 Recommended three two-hour sessions or longer practice
🧭 Guidance to help guests feel safe and in control
🏔️ Faster improvement with regular instruction and support

Equipment Hire

Our equipment hire service helps guests travel more easily without carrying all their own gear. Visitors can arrange the main skiing items they need for comfort, movement, and safety during the trip.

Travel Easy

Best For

Guests who do not own full skiing equipment or want lighter travel.

Service Style

Practical, convenient, and suitable for short or extended holiday stays.

Main Benefit

Less travel stress and easier access to important ski equipment.

⛷️ Skis, boots, helmet, goggles, and ski poles
🧤 Clothing support such as gloves, base layers, and jackets
🧳 Optional items like ski bags and travel accessories
❄️ Better convenience for guests who want a simple trip

Ski / Snowboard Servicing

Proper servicing and fitting are important for comfort and performance. Our service guidance helps guests understand equipment fit, board size, and boot choice so they can avoid unnecessary difficulty on the snow.

Fit & Support

Best For

Guests who want more comfort, better fit, and more control while riding.

Service Style

Helpful fitting advice with attention to size, movement, and equipment balance.

Main Benefit

Better performance and fewer problems caused by poor equipment fit.

👢 Boot fitting advice for snowboard and ski comfort
📏 Board and boot size guidance for better balance
🛠️ Practical support for smoother performance on snow
✅ Better control by avoiding equipment that is too large or unsuitable
